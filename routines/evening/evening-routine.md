# Evening Routine

Wind down intentionally to rest well and prepare for tomorrow.

## Checklist

- [ ] Review today's accomplishments
- [ ] Check off completed tasks from the day
- [ ] Prepare tomorrow's to-do list (top 3 priorities)
- [ ] Lay out clothes for tomorrow
- [ ] Tidy workspace / living area (10-minute reset)
- [ ] Personal hygiene (skincare, brush teeth)
- [ ] Screen-free time (reading, journaling, stretching)
- [ ] Lights out at target bedtime

## Notes

- Start this routine 60-90 minutes before target bedtime
- The "10-minute reset" keeps things from piling up over the week
- Journaling or reflection helps process the day
