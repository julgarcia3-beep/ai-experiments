# Monthly Review

A deeper check-in to track progress and set direction. Do this on the 1st of each month.

## Reflect on Last Month

- [ ] What were my biggest wins?
- [ ] What challenges did I face?
- [ ] Which habits stuck? Which ones dropped off?
- [ ] What would I do differently?

## Review Goals

- [ ] Review last month's goals - how many did I hit?
- [ ] What helped me succeed on the ones I completed?
- [ ] What blocked me on the ones I missed?

## Set Goals for This Month

- [ ] Define 3-5 monthly goals (specific and measurable)
- [ ] Break each goal into weekly milestones
- [ ] Identify resources or support needed

## Routine Check-In

- [ ] Are my current routines working? What needs adjusting?
- [ ] Add, remove, or modify routine items as needed
- [ ] Update any templates based on what I've learned

## Notes

- Set aside 45-60 minutes for this
- Look at the big picture, not just tasks
- Celebrate progress, even if it's small
