# Weekly Review

Reflect on the past week and plan the next one. Best done on Sunday.

## Review the Past Week

- [ ] What went well this week?
- [ ] What didn't go as planned?
- [ ] What did I learn?
- [ ] Did I stick to my routines? (Be honest, not harsh)

## Plan the Coming Week

- [ ] Review calendar for upcoming events and deadlines
- [ ] Set 3-5 goals for the week
- [ ] Schedule focused work blocks
- [ ] Identify any prep needed (groceries, materials, etc.)

## Maintenance

- [ ] Clean and organize workspace
- [ ] Review and update task lists
- [ ] Check in on monthly goals - still on track?
- [ ] Batch any errands or admin tasks

## Notes

- Keep this to 30-45 minutes
- Be specific with goals (not "exercise more" but "gym Monday, Wednesday, Friday")
